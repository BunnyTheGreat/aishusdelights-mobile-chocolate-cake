Chocolate Cake




Chocolate cake is a beloved treat for people around the world. This recipe shows how you can make a delicious, moist, and luscious cake with a few ingredients. This delectable treat is a must have for any day, whether a holiday, a party, a birthday, or even for you to just enjoy yourself! I really hope you enjoy this cake, as it is my favorite dessert, and I hope its yours, too.




Ingredients for the Cake:

    2 cups all-purpose flour

    2 cups sugar

    3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

    2 teaspoons baking powder

    1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

    1 teaspoon salt

    1 teaspoon espresso powder

    1 cup milk

    1/2 cup vegetable oil

    2 large eggs

    2 teaspoons vanilla extract

    1 cup boiling water



Ingredients for Frosting:

    1½ cups butter 3 sticks, softened

    1 cup unsweetened cocoa

    5 cups confectioner’s sugar

    ½ cup milk

    2 teaspoons vanilla extract

    ½ teaspoon espresso powder
